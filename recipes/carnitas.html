Slow Cooker Beef Carnitas

This is a very good slow cooker recipe. These carnitas are particularly good served with homemade guacamole and fresh cooked tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs Beef Chuck Roast
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Onion - Sliced
  • 1 Green Pepper - Sliced
  • 1 Red Pepper - Sliced
  • 1 (4 oz) can Undrained Diced Green Chilis

Instructions

  1. Brown beef roast in a skillet (Optional)
  2. Place roast in a slow cooker
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together chili powder, salt, pepper, cumin, paprika, garlic powder and onion powder
  4. sprinkle spice mixture over the roast
  5. Add onion, green pepper, red pepper and green chilis
  6. Cook on low for 7-8 hours
  7. Shred beef and serve on tortillas
