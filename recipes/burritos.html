South of the Border Smothered Burritos

This is a fairly easy and fast recipe to make delicious smothered burritos. These can be served by themselves or with a side of lettuce, tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 2 cans (7 oz each) Green Chili Salsa
  • 1 can (15 oz) Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 can (15 oz) Refried Beans
  • 12 (10-12") Flour Tortillas
  • 1/2 lb Jack Cheese
  • 1/2 lb Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 cup Chopped Onion

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, brown ground beef
  2. While the beef is cooking, combine the green chile salsa and chicken soup in a saucepan and warm over medium heat
  3. Meanwhile, heat the beans in a separate pan and soften the tortillas
  4. Once the beef is ready and the other ingredients are warm, fill the tortillas with the following: 1 tablespoon each of meat, salsa mixture and beans
  5. Add onions and cheese
  6. Roll tortillas and place in a casserole dish
  7. Top with remaining salsa, onions and cheese
  8. Bake in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes until bubbly
